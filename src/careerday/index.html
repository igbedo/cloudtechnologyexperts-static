What is Career Day?

Cloud Technology Experts Career Day is a monthly event dedicated to discussing real career opportunities in tech and how to break into them. You’ll leave with clarity on the best pathway for your goals and a practical next-step plan.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about Career Day.

Is Career Day free?

Yes. Registration is required so we can send venue details and event reminders.

Do I need experience?

No. Career Day is designed for beginners and career changers.

Can workforce programs (TWC/WIOA) cover training?

In many cases, yes. We’ll explain how the process works and what documentation is needed.

What happens after Career Day?

You’ll receive next-step guidance and can apply to the pathway that fits your goals.
