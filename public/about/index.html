About Cloud Technology Experts

Helping beginners break into Cloud, Cybersecurity & DevOps — one job-ready graduate at a time.

Who We Are

Cloud Technology Experts is a cloud training and consulting company focused on one mission: turn motivated beginners into confident, job-ready cloud and DevOps professionals.

We deliver both online and in-person training to students around the world, combining hands-on labs, real-world projects, instructor support, and career mentorship.

Whether you’re starting with zero IT experience or leveling up an existing career, we provide a clear path from where you are now to your first (or next) role in tech.

Our Training Categories

1. Zero-to-Hero Career Programs

These programs are designed for candidates with little or no IT background who want a tech career. As a career school, we train students for entry-level roles in:

  • Cloud Computing
  • DevOps & Site Reliability
  • Fullstack Application Development

Most learners complete the full Zero-to-Hero journey in about 5 months, graduating with the skills and portfolio to pursue their first tech job. You don’t need a degree — just willingness to learn and commit to the process.

2. Online, Self-Paced Training

For working professionals who prefer to learn on their own schedule, we also offer self-paced online courses. This is a great option if you already have some experience and want to focus on specific skills or certifications.

  • Learn anytime, from anywhere
  • Revisit lessons whenever you need
  • Purchase courses individually or for your team

3. Corporate Training

We partner with organizations to upskill their workforce in modern cloud-native technologies, including:

  • Microservices application development
  • Docker and Kubernetes administration (CKA curriculum)
  • Docker and Kubernetes security (CKS curriculum)
  • AWS Solutions Architect (Associate & Professional)
  • AWS DevOps Professional

4. Weekend Short Courses

Weekend courses are remote instructor-led classes designed for busy professionals. If you work full-time and still want to move forward, these courses let you:

  • Learn in focused weekend blocks
  • Prepare for certifications like CKA and CKS
  • Strengthen your skills without disrupting your 9–5

Why Cloud Technology Experts?

Fundamental & In-Demand Skills

We teach what employers actually use: cloud, DevOps, Kubernetes, automation, and more. Our curriculum is continuously updated to match industry needs.

Project-Based, Portfolio-First

Our training includes real projects so you graduate with a portfolio that proves your skills, not just a certificate.

“On-the-Job” Style Teaching

We don’t teach topics in isolation. We show how everything fits together in real environments, so your first job feels familiar, not overwhelming.

Structured Practice & Accountability

You’ll follow a schedule of exercises, labs, and activities that help you master each piece step by step, with support from instructors and mentors.

Trusted by the world's best companies

Our instructors have several years of professional experience working with leading organizations. We bring that experience directly into the classroom, so you benefit from real-world knowledge and best practices.
