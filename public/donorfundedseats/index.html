For Students

How to Qualify for a Donor-Funded Seat

Donor-funded seats are limited and designed for students who show strong commitment and follow-through. We want donors to see real outcomes, and we want students to truly benefit from the opportunity.

  • Complete the application with a clear career goal
  • Commit to weekly attendance and lab completion
  • Participate consistently and communicate if challenges arise
  • Follow professional conduct guidelines within the cohort

Some sponsorships may include certification vouchers depending on donor preferences and funding level.

Yes. We support employer partnerships where companies sponsor seats and get early access to interview job-ready graduates.

Students are placed into the next available cohort based on track capacity, readiness, and seat availability.
